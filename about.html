About Me

My journey as a self-taught developer started during the last week of November, 2021 when i wrote my first "Hello World". I have intensely enjoyed learning from scratch, not just how to code but creative problem solving techniques. All of these and more have inspired me to take a step back, and revamp my entire professional life. Doing this, i have taken a class on Agile Project management to fully understand the life-cycle of software developement and how important every step is, from concept to development and delivery. I have also taken a class to develop my soft skills and an effective team work process. When i am not coding, I enjoy listening to music and just letting myself soak in the message the artist is passing. I also enjoy traveling and getting lost in new cities.
